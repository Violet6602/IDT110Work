Stupid Ball

Rules

  1. Play with a ping pong ball
  2. Players sit in chairs facing each other
  3. Players hit the ball by using their hands
  4. One player serves
  5. If the ball bounces and then goes past the oppsing player, the server gets a point and serves again
  6. If the serve goes out of bounds, does not bounce, bounces 4 times (becmoming dead), or is blocked, then the server serves again
  7. If the receiving player hits the ball back and scores, they get a point and get to serve
  8. Once the match is at game point, the player not at game point gets to serve
  9. Also at game point, the serving player switches if the receiving player is able to block twice
